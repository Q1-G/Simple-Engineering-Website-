About Us

At G.V.C Engineering & Maintenance, we bring over three decades of unwavering commitment and expertise in the field of engineering and the built environment. Situated in Randburg, Johannesburg, our reach extends across the entirety of South Africa. With a rich legacy of 30 years, we specialize in the meticulous maintenance of commercial buildings, offering a comprehensive suite of services that includes structural engineering, building maintenance, shop fitting, solar installations and renovations. Our philosophy is anchored in excellence and honesty, values that resonate in every project we undertake. At G.V.C, we prioritize customer satisfaction, ensuring that our clients receive not only top-tier engineering solutions but also a partnership built on trust and reliability. Additionally, our commitment to safety is paramount. We meticulously follow health and safety practices, abiding by industry laws and regulations to ensure a secure working environment at all times. Choose G.V.C for a blend of experience, professionalism, and a steadfast commitment to quality.
